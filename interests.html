Interests and Hobbies

I've had quite an interesting and extensive exposure to the world through my time in the United States Army. Through my experiences, I've learned that I want to pursue a highly technical career in the field of Software Engineering and Programming. My fascination with computers began at an early age, when my Dad brought home our very first desktop computer. It was an i286, and I loved having him show me the things that he would be working on. Later, while I was in the Army, I met a friend who taught me how to choose and build my own computer hardware from scratch. I’ve been building my own desktop computers since then just for fun, but it wasn’t until I had my friends asking me why I don’t just study computers in college and make a career out of my interests that I decided to look deeper into it. After some research, I realized there wasn’t much business in simply building computers any more so I decided to see what other areas of the computer world might interest me. I took a class on intro to programming and algorithms using the C language and I loved every bit of it. So, I decided to pursue a degree in Computer Science. And that’s how I turned my interests and hobby into a budding career.
